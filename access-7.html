Improving Design

Sip #7: Color Contrast

Color contrast is the contrast between the foreground and background colors. A contrast ratio of at least 4.5 is recommended for an easier viewing experience for users.

There are many online contrast checkers that can assist with checking the contrast ratio on your website. Try One.
